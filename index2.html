02 · Company Overview

Ui Hub is a focused e-commerce business built around high-demand technology products.

About the company

Ui Hub Computer & Electronics Trading FZ-LLC specializes in technology products and lifestyle electronics sold through major online marketplaces in the Middle East. The business focuses on sourcing high-demand products with strong margins and scaling them through efficient marketplace fulfillment systems.

Core Product Categories

  • Graphic Drawing Tablets
  • E-Readers
  • Electronics Accessories
  • Smart Watches
  • Portable Audio Speakers
  • Robotic Vacuums
  • Lifestyle Products such as D1 Milano Watches

These categories combine strong demand, scalable online sales, and healthy margins.
